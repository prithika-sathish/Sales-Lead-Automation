# Company: SubScale

## PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION
SubScale is a B2B SaaS platform providing subscription billing infrastructure, recurring payment orchestration, invoicing, and revenue analytics for companies with complex or scaling monetization models. It is API-first and designed to handle usage-based, hybrid, and global billing workflows.
